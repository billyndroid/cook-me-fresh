About Us

Cook Me Fresh was created to bring real, practical cooking support into people’s homes. Founded by Gavin Akers—a chef with over 20 years of experience—our mission is to help you feel confident cooking fresh, healthy meals, whether you’re shopping on a budget or using the very best ingredients available.

Gav’s journey in food includes co-running a restaurant in British Columbia, Canada, co-owning the popular Flagstones Tapas Bar, running his own bakery, and gaining hands-on experience in butchery. He’s also delivered public food demonstrations for Rhondda Cynon Taf (RCT) Council in Ynysangharad War Memorial Park, sharing his love of cooking with the wider community.

Whether it’s one-to-one support or small group demonstrations, everything is tailored to your kitchen, your lifestyle, and what you actually want to eat.

Based in South Wales, Cook Me Fresh focuses on local, seasonal ingredients and smart shopping advice—so you can cook well without the stress.

At the heart of it all, it’s not about being perfect. It’s about feeling good, eating well, and enjoying the process.

Our Services

Personalised Meal Planning

Tailored cooking support built around your tastes, needs, and lifestyle.

  • Starts with a free 30-minute home consultation
    • Includes a short questionnaire to cover:
      • Allergies
      • Likes and dislikes
      • Dietary needs and preferences
    • We’ll discuss the number of meals you want to cook and how long you'd like the session to be (typically 2–3 hours)
  • After the consultation:
    • We will plan a selection of personalised recipes
    • You’ll receive a full shopping list with advice on where to find the best value, quality ingredients locally

Food Demonstrations

Engaging, in-person cooking demonstrations that bring people together through good food and practical guidance.

  • Ideal for community events, local organisations, wellbeing groups, schools, or staff wellness days
  • Designed to suit your space and audience, whether it’s a small venue or larger group setting
  • Learn how to prepare nutritious, affordable meals using local, seasonal ingredients
  • Demos include practical cooking tips, easy ingredient swaps, and simple methods to eat well on a budget
  • We highlight where to shop, how to stretch your spend, and how to get great quality ingredients without overpaying
  • Sessions can be tailored to specific themes such as family cooking, vegetarian meals, batch prep, or heart-healthy eating

We has previously delivered food demonstrations for RCT Council at Ynysangharad War Memorial Park, engaging the local community with simple, flavour-packed recipes and real-world shopping advice. Prices start at £400, with packages available for larger or recurring sessions.

Let's Cook Together

A fun, hands-on cooking session for small groups who want to learn, share, and enjoy food in a relaxed setting.

  • Ideal for friends, families, private groups, or teams looking for something different
  • Hosted in your kitchen or a chosen venue—we bring the energy and guidance, you bring the crew
  • Everyone gets involved, learning practical skills while cooking simple, delicious meals together
  • Sessions can be tailored to your group’s tastes—like pasta from scratch, budget-friendly favourites, batch cooking, or healthy comfort food
  • Perfect for groups of 4–8 people (flexible depending on space and setup)
  • Includes a shopping list with advice on where to get the best quality ingredients at the best price

Price: £25 per person (plus cost of ingredients)

Duration: Typically 2–4 hours, depending on the menu and group size

We’ll cook, chat, laugh, and eat—no lectures, no pressure—just proper food, done your way.
